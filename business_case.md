## Business Problem

A retail company observed increasing sales but declining profitability.
The objective was to identify revenue leakage drivers across pricing,
product performance, returns, and regional operations.

## Approach

- Built relational data model using SQL.
- Conducted SKU-level profitability analysis.
- Evaluated discount impact on margins.
- Quantified financial losses caused by returns.
- Compared revenue vs servicing cost by region.
- Designed an interactive Power BI dashboard.

## Key Insights

- High discount tiers significantly reduced profitability.
- Several high-volume products were structurally loss-making.
- Operational returns contributed measurable financial leakage.
- Certain regions showed cost inefficiencies relative to revenue.

## Business Impact

This analysis provides decision support for pricing optimization,
product strategy, and operational improvements.
